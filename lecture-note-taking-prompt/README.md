# 🎓 Lecture Note-Taking Prompt

## Why This Was Built

I was learning from video lectures — but every time I wanted to turn a transcript
into proper study notes, I had to re-explain my style, my format, and my level
to AI — over and over.

That was redundant. So I built this prompt once, saved it, and now I reuse it forever.

---

## What It Does

You give it a lecture transcript and a module name.
It gives you complete, beginner-friendly study notes — instantly.

Every output includes a plain-language overview of the topic, key concept
explanations with definitions, important details to remember, a real-world
analogy or example, and a clean summary at the end.

If there's a practice quiz after the lecture, it handles that too — with
answers and explanations written for a complete beginner.

---

## Who It's For

- 🐣 Beginners who want to actually *understand* what they watched, not just rewatch it
- 📚 Self-learners who study from video courses and want structured notes
- 🗂️ Anyone who wants to build a personal knowledge base from lecture transcripts

---

## How To Use

**Step 1** — Copy the full prompt from [`prompt.md`](./prompt.md)

**Step 2** — Paste it into Claude (claude.ai)

**Step 3** — Follow with your content like this:

```
Course: [Your course name]
Module: [Module name / Lecture title]

LECTURE SCRIPT:
[Paste your transcript here]

PRACTICE QUIZ:       ← include this only if there is one
[Paste quiz questions here]
```

**Step 4** — Get your full study notes in Markdown ✅
